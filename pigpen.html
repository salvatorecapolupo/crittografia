PIG PEN INSTRUCTIONS

What is a Pig Pen Cipher?

The Pig Pen Cipher is a type of simple substitution cipher in which letters of the alphabet are switched with symbols.

How to Solve

When solving the Pig Pen Cipher, the player is given an encrypted message and a cipher. To decrypt the message, the player will find the symbol from the encryped message on the cipher to find the decrypted letter.
