CAESER CIPHER INSTRUCTIONS

What is a Caeser Cipher?

The Caeser cipher is a type of shift cipher and is known to many as one of the most simple and well known of ciphers.The shift in most Caeser Ciphers is three letters back(ex: e = b).

How to Solve

When solving the Caeser cipher, the player is given an encrypted message and a cipher. To decrypt the message, the player will find the encryped letter on the inner circle of the cipher to find the decryped letter on the outer circle.

Example

encrypted message: zxbpbo

decrypted message: caeser
